SHRIJI VANDAN

posted on 21st september 2015

Sprawled over 10,000 sq. ft. and G+3 levels, Shriji Vandan is a very well known commercial project. With the location right on the cross road. It has all a retailer or a businessman can wish for. To accommodate the needs of every retailer or a businessman, it has road facing shops & retail spaces starting from 350 sq. ft. With Retail Shops, food courts & Offices, this will definately attract a major footfall.

AMENITIES

  • Ultra modern Commercial project situated on a prime location.
  • Beautiful Elevation having 14 shops, 7 offices, 1 huge hall and a terrace office.
  • Hall on third floor are best suited for banks, restraunts, gym e.t.c.
  • Every shop/office/hall has attached Bathroom with 24 hours running water.
  • Elevator
  • Ceramic tiles and flooring for every Shops & Offices.
  • Concealed wiring with enough points for electric, telephone & tv.
  • Basement parking area.
  • Distemper paint for shop and offices & good quality colour paint for exterior.
  • attractive elevation with full road side vision for every shop and offices.

CONDITIONS

  • Rights regarding building plans, specifications and prices are reserved with the developer and are subject to change without any notice.
  • Civil work changes regarding exterior elevation and finishes are strictly prohibited during construction and shall be controlled by building association in future.
  • All features and finishes where purchaser is given the option to select the style and/or colour shall be from the Developers predetermined standard selections.
  • Posession of premise will be given against receipt of full payment.
  • Booking cancellation charges will be applicable @18% of the booking amount.
  • Extra expense shall be charged to the purchaser for the following services:
    • Stamp duty and registration fees of sale deed.
    • Power connection.
    • Maintenance Deposit.
    • Municipal expenses.
    • Service tax/VAT as applicable
    • Extra work if any.
